A Little About Us

Here at Bountiful Foods we are committed to having the freshest and most delicious fruits in the state. Almost all of our fruits are straight from the farm and onto your plate. Our owners personally make sure that your food and fruit is delicious and healthy. Whether you're a local Californian or just seeing all this great state has to offer, come find us at our store in Carlsbad or one of our food trucks!
